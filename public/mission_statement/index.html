Our Mission

The purpose of the ALUNA Research Fellowship is to provide high-school students that have a strong interest in bioinformatics research a platform to collaborate on research projects and eventually publish their research in prestigious high-school or undergraduate journals. At ALUNA, research fellows will have the opportunity to become immersed in one or more of these fields and will aim to produce at least one publishable paper by the end of the summer. For more information about each topic and to view examples of previously published research in these fields, visit the topical research page.
